Reconstruction of 3rd-Century Japanese Phonetics

Based on the Wei Zhi Woren Zhuan (魏志倭人伝)

This study reconstructs the phonetic values of Japanese place names and ethnonyms recorded in the Chinese historical text Wei Zhi Woren Zhuan, using Old Chinese phonological systems and comparative linguistic analysis.
