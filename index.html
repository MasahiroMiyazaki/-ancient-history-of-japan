Reconstruction of 3rd-Century Japanese Phonetics

Based on the Wei Zhi Woren Zhuan (魏志倭人伝)

This paper reconstructs the phonetic values of Japanese place names as recorded by Chinese historians in the 3rd century, using Old Chinese reconstruction systems and comparative phonology.
